Salt Lake City Temple

The Salt lake City Temple, is the largest LDS temple by floor area. Dedicated in 1893, it is the sixth temple completed by the church, requiring 40 years to complete. The temple is considered the house of God and is reserved for special ceremonies for practicing Latter-Day Saints.
