Site Name

The site name of this project will be called WeatherTown.com. It will contain all the wheatear status and forecast for tree different towns.

Site pourpuse

Than main purpose of the WeatherTown website will be bring information of the current weather conditions and other town information or relevant articles/content of the towns alfa beta and zeta. This objective will be fulfilled through the description of each town, location, time zone and a detailed information. Another objective or purpose will be to provide and promote the most important places in each town, maps and what to do.

Target audience in scenarios

The target audience of the WheaterTown.com web site is described below:

  • Who: Tourists and business people. And others who are interested in our towns
  • Age: 14-100
  • Income: As those places are very expensive the income level will typical be between $50000 and above annually
  • Experience: Travels with a high expectation and knowledge of types of climates/li>
